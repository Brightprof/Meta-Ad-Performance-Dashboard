# Meta-Ad-Performance-Dashboard
Interactive Meta Ad Performance Dashboard analyzing $2.5M in ad spend across Facebook &amp; Instagram. Delivers real-time insights on campaign ROI, audience demographics, and conversion funnels to drive data-backed marketing decisions.

## 📊 Project Overview
This project features an interactive **Meta Ad Performance Dashboard** designed to track, analyze, and optimize digital marketing campaigns across Facebook and Instagram. Built to bridge the gap between complex marketing data and actionable business strategy, this dashboard provides marketing teams and stakeholders with real-time insights into audience engagement, budget efficiency, and conversion funnel performance.

## 🚀 Key Business Metrics Delivered
The dashboard tracks and visualizes critical marketing Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) to drive high-level decision-making:
* **Top-of-Funnel Visibility:** Evaluates brand awareness through **216K total Impressions** and **25.4K Clicks**.
* **Conversion Efficiency:** Tracks deep funnel actions with a **5.21% Conversion Rate** and **1.3K Purchases**.
* **Budget Optimization:** Monitors a **$2.5M Total Budget** with a granular breakdown of an average budget per campaign at **$50.7K**.
* **Engagement Health:** Measures audience interest via a strong **11.76% Click-Through Rate (CTR)** and **13.56% Engagement Rate**.

---

## 🔍 Key Insights & Analytical Features

### 1. Demographic & Geographic Deep-Dives
* **Gender & Age Breakdown:** Pinpoints exact target demographics. For instance, the data reveals that **Female users drive the highest engagement (43%)**, peaking sharply around the **20–25 age bracket**.
* **Geographic Performance:** Ranks top-performing regions, highlighting the **United States (2.3K clicks)** and the **United Kingdom (1.1K clicks)** as core revenue-driving markets.

### 2. Campaign & Ad Type Granularity
* **Format Evaluation:** Features a matrix analysis comparing **Video, Stories, Image, and Carousel** ads. Video ads lead the pack with a stellar **11.78% CTR** and a **13.67% Engagement Rate**.
* **Temporal Trends:** Displays **Weekly and Hourly Clicks Trends** alongside an **Analysis by Month** calendar view to identify peak conversion times and optimize ad scheduling (dayparting).

### 3. High-Fidelity Interactivity
* Includes dynamic multi-select slicers for **Dynamic Metrics, Campaign Name, and Target Interest**, allowing users to instantly isolate variables and drill down into specific campaign segments.

## 💡 Business Impact
By deploying this dashboard, an organization can:
1. **Reduce Ad Spend Waste:** Instantly identify underperforming ad formats or demographics (e.g., lower-performing ad types or age groups) to reallocate budget dynamically.
2. **Maximize ROI:** Capitalize on peak engagement hours and high-converting regions (like the US and UK) to boost the overall **0.61% Purchase Rate**.
3. **Data-Driven Agility:** Eliminate manual reporting by providing stakeholders with a self-service tool to monitor campaign health instantly.
