# 📊 Meta Ad Performance Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The Meta Ad Performance Dashboard is an interactive analytics report designed to evaluate digital marketing campaigns across Facebook and Instagram. The dashboard provides marketing stakeholders, performance marketers, and business leaders with actionable insights into audience engagement, budget efficiency, geographic reach, and conversion funnel performance to support data-driven ad spend optimization.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

In digital marketing, inefficient ad spend allocation can lead to low conversion rates, high customer acquisition costs, and wasted budget. 

This project aims to:

- Evaluate the visibility and engagement health of ad campaigns
- Identify top-performing ad formats to maximize return on investment (ROI)
- Understand audience engagement across key demographics (gender and age)
- Analyze regional and temporal performance to optimize ad scheduling and targeting
- Support strategic decision-making through clear, real-time marketing intelligence

## 💡 Key Insights

### 1. High Funnel Engagement

The dashboard reveals strong audience interest, highlighted by a notable **11.76% Click-Through Rate (CTR)** and a **13.56% Engagement Rate** across all campaigns.

### 2. Video Ads Dominance

Video ads stand out as the most effective creative format, driving **15K Impressions** and generating the highest individual **CTR (11.78%)** and **Conversion Rate (5.56%)**.

### 3. Core Demographic Drivers

The primary audience segment is heavily concentrated among **Female users (43%)**, with engagement peaking sharply in the early-career **20–25 age bracket**.

### 4. Primary Geographic Markets

The **United States (2.3K clicks)** and the **United Kingdom (1.1K clicks)** stand out as the dominant revenue-driving regions, significantly outpacing other international markets.

### 5. Stable Purchase Bottom-Funnel

While top-of-funnel engagement is high, the overall **Purchase Rate stands at 0.61%**, netting **1.3K Purchases** from the total ad spend.

---

## 📌 Recommendations

### Optimize Creative Asset Allocation

Allocate a larger share of the budget to **Video and Stories formats**, as they demonstrate superior CTR and Conversion Rates compared to standard Images and Carousels.

### Refine Demographic Targeting

Tailor future creative assets and copywriting specifically to **Female audiences aged 20–25** to capitalize on the highest-performing demographic segment.

### Geographic Budget Prioritization

Focus the primary portion of the **$2.5M budget** on the **US and UK markets** to maximize click returns, while implementing localized testing strategies for emerging markets like Canada and India.

### Implement Strategic Dayparting

Leverage the **Hourly Clicks Trend** insight to schedule ad delivery during peak daily activity windows, reducing ad spend waste during low-engagement hours.

### Funnel Conversion Optimization

Address the gap between the high **11.76% CTR** and the **0.61% Purchase Rate** by auditing post-click landing pages to improve checkout friction and overall conversion performance.
